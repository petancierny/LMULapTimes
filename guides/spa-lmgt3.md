## Turn-by-Turn

### Turn 1
- Braking point: Brake around the 100m board; hard straight-line braking.
- Turn-in point: Turn in late with progressive steering.
- Apex: Late apex on the inside kerb; prioritize exit speed.

### Turn 2
- Braking point: Flat; use the right side on entry.
- Turn-in point: Cut the left-hand entry kerb for Eau Rouge.
- Apex: Inside kerb; keep momentum for the climb.

### Turn 3
- Braking point: No brake; crest the right-hander with commitment.
- Turn-in point: Late apex on the right; avoid heavy kerb use.
- Apex: Light touch of inside kerb; stay settled over the crest.

### Turn 4
- Braking point: Flat if line is correct.
- Turn-in point: Blind left over the crest; keep steering straight.
- Apex: Inside kerb; use exit kerb on the right within limits.

### Turn 5
- Braking point: Brake around the 100m board; down to 3rd/2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for the first right of Les Combes.
- Apex: Climb the entry kerb; avoid being greedy.

### Turn 6
- Braking point: Release brake early; set up the left.
- Turn-in point: Use the inside kerb to help rotation.
- Apex: Inside kerb; keep the car left for the next right.

### Turn 7
- Braking point: No heavy brake; carry speed.
- Turn-in point: Turn in early for the right; avoid excess kerb.
- Apex: Inside kerb; throttle early for the run to Bruxelles.

### Turn 8
- Braking point: Modulate braking to keep minimum speed up.
- Turn-in point: Aim for a clean rotation to avoid running wide.
- Apex: Tight apex; patience before throttle.

### Turn 9
- Braking point: Lift or light brake from the right side.
- Turn-in point: Late apex; keep steering input steady.
- Apex: Inside kerb if stable; power on just before apex.

### Turn 10
- Braking point: Brake/turn at halfway of the entry kerb; 3rd/4th gear.
- Turn-in point: Turn in as you brake for Pouhon.
- Apex: First left apex; keep the car balanced.

### Turn 11
- Braking point: Carry speed; commitment required.
- Turn-in point: Second left of Pouhon; smooth inputs.
- Apex: Second apex; build throttle on exit.

### Turn 14
- Braking point: Brake at the start of the left entry kerb; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Square off the right-hander to prioritize exit.
- Apex: Inside kerb; early throttle to the exit kerb.

### Turn 15
- Braking point: Flat; no brake.
- Turn-in point: Turn in just before the kerb ends for Paul Frere.
- Apex: Use all exit for the run to Blanchimont.

### Turn 16
- Braking point: Flat; position to the right on entry.
- Turn-in point: Late apex for Blanchimont; smooth steering.
- Apex: Avoid heavy inside kerb use; keep speed high.
