# Sebring - LMGT3 Track Guide

## Source Coverage
- Sources: https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/le-mans-ultimate-sebring-international-raceway-circuit-guide/, https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/the-2025-track-guide-list-for-le-mans-ultimate/
- Patch: Not stated in source (guide published 2024-11-08)
- Track layout: 17 turns

## Turn-by-Turn

### Turn 1
- Braking point: Brake 1â€“2 car lengths before the end of the pit wall; down to 4th.
- Turn-in point: Early turn-in with trail brake; apex is blind.
- Apex: Late apex just behind the black board on the inside fence.

### Turn 2
- Braking point: Flat.
- Turn-in point: Short right kink; look ahead to T3.
- Apex: Minimal apex; keep the line tidy.

### Turn 3
- Braking point: Brake between the 300/200 boards or first two white lines; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in just after the 100 board; gentle input for late apex.
- Apex: Climb the end of the inside kerb to rotate the car.

### Turn 4
- Braking point: Carry from T3; light throttle modulation.
- Turn-in point: Short right; keep tight to open T5.
- Apex: Use inside kerb at the back of the kerb.

### Turn 5
- Braking point: Brake just after the white/blue kerb ends on the right; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in after downshift; aim for late apex.
- Apex: Late apex; avoid the tall inside kerb; exit to the long straight.

### Turn 6
- Braking point: Flat; no braking.
- Turn-in point: Stay right, then drift left as the tarmac changes color.
- Apex: Long right bend; shortest line without scrub.

### Turn 7
- Braking point: Brake just after the first white line; down to 1st.
- Turn-in point: Trail brake and rotate 2 car lengths after the 100 board.
- Apex: Inside kerb; avoid the yellow sausage kerb.

### Turn 8
- Braking point: Flat.
- Turn-in point: Smooth right bend.
- Apex: Short apex; keep steering minimal.

### Turn 9
- Braking point: Flat.
- Turn-in point: Smooth left bend.
- Apex: Shortest line; avoid scrubbing speed.

### Turn 10
- Braking point: Brake at the white line before the 300 board; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in as the outside blue/white kerb begins.
- Apex: Late apex behind the inside kerb; manage bumps on exit.

### Turn 11
- Braking point: Flat.
- Turn-in point: Keep a tight line; avoid apex kerbs that unsettle the car.
- Apex: Long left; shortest line through the corner.

### Turn 12
- Braking point: No brake; short kink.
- Turn-in point: Let the car run out to open T13.
- Apex: Do not apex the kerb.

### Turn 13
- Braking point: Brake when parallel to the outside white line; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Trail brake and turn in over the bumps.
- Apex: Slight cut of the inside kerb; focus on exit speed.

### Turn 14
- Braking point: Flat.
- Turn-in point: Easy left; avoid cutting the inside kerb.
- Apex: Stay close to the inside kerb without climbing it.

### Turn 15
- Braking point: Brake at the 300 board; down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Turn between the 200/100 boards; trail brake.
- Apex: Inside kerb; avoid too much speed to keep line for the left.

### Turn 16
- Braking point: Light brake a car length after the T15 kerb ends; down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in when the inside marshal post is visible.
- Apex: Inside kerb; be patient before throttle for rotation.

### Turn 17
- Braking point: Brake at the 200 board; trail brake to the late apex near the Mobil 1 banners.
- Turn-in point: Turn in around the 200 board and continue turning to the inside wall.
- Apex: Late apex under the bridge; smooth throttle on exit.
