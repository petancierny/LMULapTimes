# Interlagos - LMGT3 Track Guide

## Source Coverage

## Turn-by-Turn

### Turn 1
- Braking point: Brake hard a couple car lengths before the end of the green wall; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in just after the bridge using trail braking.
- Apex: Left apex at the inside kerb where green astroturf meets grass.

### Turn 2
- Braking point: Continuation from T1; no extra brake needed.
- Turn-in point: Stay left to open the right-hand part of Senna S.
- Apex: Mid apex on the right-hander; smooth throttle to avoid running wide.

### Turn 3
- Braking point: Flat in LMGT3.
- Turn-in point: Gentle turn-in; keep line tight.
- Apex: Stay close to inside kerb to shorten the line.

### Turn 4
- Braking point: Brake just after the marshal post on the right; down to 2nd/3rd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for the left; be patient as camber drops away.
- Apex: Climb the inside kerb as the yellow sausage starts.

### Turn 5
- Braking point: Flat from T4 exit.
- Turn-in point: Gentle left curve; keep smooth steering.
- Apex: Use full road to keep the line smooth.

### Turn 6
- Braking point: Brake/turn together just after the last GoodYear board; down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Aim for the first right-hand apex before the sausage kerb ends.
- Apex: Hold tight to the inside kerb; maintenance throttle to avoid oversteer.

### Turn 7
- Braking point: Short carryover from T6; no new heavy braking.
- Turn-in point: Re-rotate for the second right apex.
- Apex: Second apex around midway of the inside sausage kerb; full throttle after.

### Turn 8
- Braking point: Brake just after the DHL board; down to 2nd/1st.
- Turn-in point: Late turn-in for the downhill right.
- Apex: Late apex near the end of the yellow sausage kerb.

### Turn 9
- Braking point: Small brake to shift weight; patience through long left.
- Turn-in point: Keep the car mid-track from T8 exit and turn in tight.
- Apex: Close to inside kerb; wait to rotate before throttle.

### Turn 10
- Braking point: Brake just before astro turf turns to grass; down to 1st.
- Turn-in point: Delay turn-in for a late apex.
- Apex: Stay with inside kerb until the sausage kerb ends.

### Turn 11
- Braking point: Flat; no braking once settled from T10.
- Turn-in point: Gentle turn-in from far right to keep full throttle.
- Apex: Light touch of inside kerb; smooth steering to avoid scrubbing.

### Turn 12
- Braking point: Brake a car length after the grass ends on the right.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for a mid apex; set early throttle.
- Apex: Apex mid inside kerb; traction on exit up the hill.

### Turn 13
- Braking point: Flat.
- Turn-in point: Run mid-track to open T14.
- Apex: No hard apex needed; keep line smooth.

### Turn 14
- Braking point: Flat.
- Turn-in point: Stay close to inside fence from kerb start to end.
- Apex: Inside kerb; open steering for the straight.
