# COTA - LMGT3 Track Guide

## Source Coverage
- Sources: https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/le-mans-ultimate-circuit-of-americas-cota-track-guide/, https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/the-2025-track-guide-list-for-le-mans-ultimate/
- Patch: Not stated in source (guide published 2025-01-13)
- Track layout: 20 turns

## Turn-by-Turn

### Turn 1
- Braking point: Brake hard around the 100m board on the uphill; trail brake as you crest.
- Turn-in point: Turn in earlier than expected from the far right because the hill hides the apex.
- Apex: Hug the inside kerb; avoid climbing it to keep the car settled.

### Turn 2
- Braking point: No brake; flat-out kink.
- Turn-in point: Let the car flow left, then aim a midline approach to set up T3.
- Apex: Short right-hand apex with minimal kerb usage.

### Turn 3
- Braking point: Flat through; no braking.
- Turn-in point: Aim for the short left apex to open T4.
- Apex: Clip the left apex while keeping two wheels inside the white line.

### Turn 4
- Braking point: Light brake and carry 4th gear into the right-hander.
- Turn-in point: Small steering input; sweep in smoothly.
- Apex: Close to the inside curb without crossing the solid white line.

### Turn 5
- Braking point: Trail brake and downshift to 3rd for the left sweep.
- Turn-in point: Flow left from T4; keep the car balanced.
- Apex: Use the inside while staying within track limits.

### Turn 6
- Braking point: Maintain 3rd; light trail brake to control speed.
- Turn-in point: Tighten the line for the double-apex right.
- Apex: Use the inside curb on the first apex, then be patient as the car drifts out.

### Turn 7
- Braking point: Approach from the right and scrub speed as needed (3rd/4th).
- Turn-in point: Turn in cleanly to the left apex and unwind early.
- Apex: Left apex with early throttle to carry momentum to T8.

### Turn 8
- Braking point: Brake at the end of the right-side red/white kerb; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in early and hug the inside kerb/astroturf.
- Apex: Inside kerb; keep two wheels inside the white line.

### Turn 9
- Braking point: Minimal brake; throttle modulation only.
- Turn-in point: Use the inside kerb to straighten the exit.
- Apex: Short left apex; keep the car settled over the crest.

### Turn 10
- Braking point: Flat; no braking needed.
- Turn-in point: Gentle left bend with smooth steering.
- Apex: Use the inside striped kerb to shorten the line.

### Turn 11
- Braking point: Brake hard at the 2nd Motul board; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in about a car length before the kerb ends for a late apex.
- Apex: Late apex; avoid the inside sausage kerb.

### Turn 12
- Braking point: Brake at the 3rd Michelin board; down from 6th to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in about a car length after the last Michelin board.
- Apex: Inside kerb dip; build throttle on exit.

### Turn 13
- Braking point: Brake at the last horizontal white line meeting the outer kerb; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in to the inside sausage while keeping outside wheels inside the line.
- Apex: Inside kerb; patience before throttle as you stay turning right.

### Turn 14
- Braking point: Light brake or lift; carry speed through the right.
- Turn-in point: Use the inside kerb and set up for T15.
- Apex: Right-hand apex on the red/white kerb.

### Turn 15
- Braking point: Brake a car length before grass turns to gravel on the right; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Wait for a late apex to straighten the exit.
- Apex: Tight inside kerb; get on power early if you hit the late apex.

### Turn 16
- Braking point: Flat or slight lift for the first right of the triple-apex.
- Turn-in point: Turn in from the left to the first right apex.
- Apex: First apex; keep the car settled for the next two.

### Turn 17
- Braking point: Gentle lift between apexes; stay smooth.
- Turn-in point: Aim for the second apex while keeping balance.
- Apex: Second apex; begin feeding throttle.

### Turn 18
- Braking point: No heavy braking; build throttle to the final apex.
- Turn-in point: Aim for the third and most important apex.
- Apex: Third apex; full exit without running wide.

### Turn 19
- Braking point: Slight lift or light brake from the right side.
- Turn-in point: Turn in to the left apex curb.
- Apex: Use the curb lightly; get back on throttle early.

### Turn 20
- Braking point: Brake just before the solid white line across the track; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Hug the inside curb on entry.
- Apex: Inside curb; strong exit using all track within limits.
