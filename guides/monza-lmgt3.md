# Monza - LMGT3 Track Guide

## Source Coverage
- Sources: https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/le-mans-ultimate-monza-circuit-guide/, https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/the-2025-track-guide-list-for-le-mans-ultimate/
- Patch: not stated in sources (track guide published 2024-11-08)
- Track layout: 11 turns

## Turn-by-Turn

### Turn 1
- Braking point: Just before the 150m board.
- Turn-in point: Aim for the astroturf patch visible in the braking phase.
- Apex: Late apex; hug the flat inside kerb and avoid the sausage kerb.

### Turn 2
- Braking point: A short dab of brake after a small throttle squirt.
- Turn-in point: Turn in to make the second apex after the dab brake.
- Apex: Second apex; avoid the inside sausage kerb.

### Turn 3
- Braking point: Source needed (guide describes it as flat-out).
- Turn-in point: Smooth steering inputs; stay close to the inside kerb.
- Apex: Hug the inside kerb through the sweep.

### Turn 4
- Braking point: Just after the 150m board and the orange block on the armco.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for a late apex just wide of the orange sausage kerb.
- Apex: Late apex just wide of the orange sausage kerb; use the striped kerb.

### Turn 5
- Braking point: Source needed (guide notes you should not need to lift fully).
- Turn-in point: Stay left on entry to open the second apex.
- Apex: Second apex; be at least half throttle and avoid the inside sausage kerb.

### Turn 6
- Braking point: Just before the orange marshal post (about 50m).
- Turn-in point: Turn in as you pass the marker.
- Apex: Where the inside armco barrier ends.

### Turn 7
- Braking point: 50m board.
- Turn-in point: Source needed.
- Apex: Mid-to-late apex using the painted part of the kerb.

### Turn 8
- Braking point: Orange-painted square on the armco; downshift to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for the first left of Ascari and use the inside kerb.
- Apex: Inside tyres over the black/yellow sausage kerb.

### Turn 9
- Braking point: Source needed (guide focuses on carrying momentum).
- Turn-in point: Source needed.
- Apex: Stay close to the inside and be mindful of the sausage kerbs.

### Turn 10
- Braking point: Source needed (power phase through the right-hand).
- Turn-in point: Settle the car; shift to 4th just before the center of the corner.
- Apex: Aggressively use the inside kerb, placing the inside wheel on the sausage kerb.

### Turn 11
- Braking point: At the 100m board in a straight line.
- Turn-in point: Turn in after the 50m board while trailing the brakes.
- Apex: Early apex; hug the inside kerb while releasing the brake.
