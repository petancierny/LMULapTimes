# Imola - LMGT3 Track Guide

## Source Coverage
- Sources: https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/le-mans-ultimate-autodromo-internazionale-enzo-e-dino-ferrari-imola-track-guide/, https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/the-2025-track-guide-list-for-le-mans-ultimate/
- Patch: Not stated in source (guide published 2024-11-08)
- Track layout: 19 turns

## Turn-by-Turn

### Turn 1
- Braking point: Brake just after the marshal post by the access road; down from 6th to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in after final downshift to set up Tamburello.
- Apex: Left-hand kink; stay right to open the chicane.

### Turn 2
- Braking point: Heavy brake from 6th to 2nd at the first marker.
- Turn-in point: Turn in just after the downshift; aim for the first sausage kerb.
- Apex: Inside sausage kerbs on the first apex of Tamburello.

### Turn 3
- Braking point: No extra brake; maintain speed through the chicane.
- Turn-in point: Set up for the second apex with smooth steering.
- Apex: Second apex over the last sausage kerbs; flat if line is right.

### Turn 4
- Braking point: No hard brake; carry speed to the final left.
- Turn-in point: Aim for a late apex to avoid running wide.
- Apex: Light inside kerb; smooth steering.

### Turn 5
- Braking point: Brake between 100m and 50m; down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for the left of Variante Villeneuve.
- Apex: Inside entry kerb with the inside wheels.

### Turn 6
- Braking point: Straighten, then brake and downshift to 2nd for the right.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for the second apex; slow-in/fast-out.
- Apex: Use the inside kerb and exit wide onto the runoff kerb.

### Turn 7
- Braking point: Brake just after the 50m board; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Mid-to-late turn-in for Tosa.
- Apex: Stay close to the inside kerb; early throttle.

### Turn 8
- Braking point: No brake for the kink; stay right over the crest.
- Turn-in point: Set up for Piratella entry.
- Apex: Short right kink; keep line stable.

### Turn 9
- Braking point: Brake just after the 50m board; down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in earlier than expected due to camber.
- Apex: Clip the inside kerb; avoid sausage kerbs.

### Turn 10
- Braking point: No hard brake; left-hand kink into Acque Minerali.
- Turn-in point: Keep the car settled to enter the sequence.
- Apex: Left kink; maintain speed.

### Turn 11
- Braking point: Brake just before the 50m board; down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in to clip the inside kerb with front-right.
- Apex: Right-hand apex; short throttle burst after.

### Turn 12
- Braking point: Light brake; down to 2nd for the chicane entry.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for the first part of the uphill chicane.
- Apex: Use the inside green kerb; avoid sausage kerbs.

### Turn 13
- Braking point: No heavy brake; focus on exit speed.
- Turn-in point: Rotate for the left-hand exit of Acque Minerali.
- Apex: Avoid yellow sausage kerbs; smooth exit to the straight.

### Turn 14
- Braking point: Brake hard as the left entry kerb starts; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in just after the bridge for the right of Variante Alta.
- Apex: Aggressively use the sausage kerb to rotate.

### Turn 15
- Braking point: No extra heavy brake; focus on smooth direction change.
- Turn-in point: Quick left with progressive throttle.
- Apex: Second sausage kerb; stay inside track limits.

### Turn 16
- Braking point: Hard brake just before the 100m board; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in slightly early to manage camber.
- Apex: Inside kerb; slow to apex for early power.

### Turn 17
- Braking point: Carry from T16; manage speed into Rivazza.
- Turn-in point: Aim for the first downhill left.
- Apex: Inside kerb; keep car balanced for the next left.

### Turn 18
- Braking point: Light brake into the second left of Rivazza.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for the final left-hander.
- Apex: Inside kerb; early throttle to the exit kerb.

### Turn 19
- Braking point: Ease onto brakes as the exit kerb ends.
- Turn-in point: Final turn-in for the last corner.
- Apex: Inside kerb; use exit kerb on the right within limits.
