# Fuji (classic) - LMGT3 Track Guide

## Source Coverage
- Sources: https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/le-mans-ultimate-fuji-speedway-track-guide/, https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/the-2025-track-guide-list-for-le-mans-ultimate/
- Patch: Not stated in source (guide published 2024-11-08)
- Track layout: 16 turns

## Turn-by-Turn

### Turn 1
- Braking point: Brake around the 150m board; a car length before it; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Delay turn-in for a late apex; trail brake for rotation.
- Apex: Late apex on the inside kerb; start throttle as you hit the dip.

### Turn 2
- Braking point: Flat.
- Turn-in point: Smooth right kink; keep car right for T3.
- Apex: Light apex; minimal kerb.

### Turn 3
- Braking point: Brake a car length before the 50m board; down to 3rd/4th.
- Turn-in point: Turn in at the 50m board; aim for green astroturf.
- Apex: Inside kerb/astroturf; avoid grass; carry momentum.

### Turn 4
- Braking point: Lift only as the road tightens; no hard brake.
- Turn-in point: Hold tight to the inside on 100R entry.
- Apex: First apex of the long right; keep the car balanced.

### Turn 5
- Braking point: Maintenance throttle; lift then reapply.
- Turn-in point: Rotate before the orange block for the second apex.
- Apex: Second apex; full throttle once the car is rotated.

### Turn 6
- Braking point: Brake just before the 50m board on the right; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in a car length before the Michelin banner.
- Apex: Slightly late apex on red/white kerb; avoid red astroturf.

### Turn 7
- Braking point: Carry from T6; light trail brake.
- Turn-in point: Exit hairpin and let car run wide.
- Apex: Hairpin exit apex; maximize track usage.

### Turn 8
- Braking point: Flat.
- Turn-in point: Mid apex on inside kerb.
- Apex: Inside kerb; let car drift mid-track.

### Turn 9
- Braking point: Flat.
- Turn-in point: Mid apex through 300R.
- Apex: Inside kerb; drift to outside for chicane.

### Turn 10
- Braking point: Brake at the Goodyear overhead board; down to 1st.
- Turn-in point: Turn in after last downshift with trail brake.
- Apex: Mid-to-late apex; inside wheels on red astro, outside on flat kerb.

### Turn 11
- Braking point: Lift and re-balance for the left of the chicane.
- Turn-in point: Turn in with a small lift; use inside kerb.
- Apex: Inside kerb; avoid grass; short-shift for traction.

### Turn 12
- Braking point: No heavy brake; let car flow to the right-hander.
- Turn-in point: Use inside kerb and cut aggressively within limits.
- Apex: Inside kerb/green astro; straighten the exit.

### Turn 13
- Braking point: Brake at the faint perpendicular white line; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in as you brake; aim for inside kerb.
- Apex: Late apex on green astro; gentle throttle to avoid over-rotation.

### Turn 14
- Braking point: Flat; keep 3rd; stay parallel to inside kerb.
- Turn-in point: Let the car drift slightly over the inside kerb.
- Apex: Long left apex; exit a car length before green astro ends.

### Turn 15
- Braking point: Brake just after red/white kerbing ends on the left; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Wait for the late apex; keep line smooth.
- Apex: Late apex; use green astro but be cautious with throttle.

### Turn 16
- Braking point: Light trail brake about two car lengths after the access road; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Patient turn-in for the blind final corner.
- Apex: Inside kerb near the white block on green astro; avoid drop-off.
