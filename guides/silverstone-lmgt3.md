# Silverstone - LMGT3 Track Guide

## Source Coverage
- Sources: https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/le-mans-ultimate-silverstone-track-guide/, https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/the-2025-track-guide-list-for-le-mans-ultimate/
- Patch: Not stated in source (guide published 2025-10-07)
- Track layout: 18 turns

## Turn-by-Turn

### Turn 1
- Braking point: Brake just after the 100m board and light grass patch; down to 4th.
- Turn-in point: Turn in around the 50m board with committed steering.
- Apex: Inside kerb between the two orange sausage kerbs.

### Turn 2
- Braking point: Flat for the left of Maggots; then brake to 4th for the right.
- Turn-in point: Turn in early from the right.
- Apex: First left apex to open the right-hander.

### Turn 3
- Braking point: Brake to 4th for the right of Maggots/Becketts.
- Turn-in point: Use the left to open the right turn.
- Apex: Light cut on the inside kerb; full throttle at apex.

### Turn 4
- Braking point: Brake as the inside green astroturf ends; down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for the left; sacrifice speed for exit.
- Apex: Cut near the orange sausage kerbs.

### Turn 5
- Braking point: Carry from T4; brief coast.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for the right; focus on exit.
- Apex: Inside kerb near the first orange block; throttle on early.

### Turn 6
- Braking point: No heavy brake; flow to Chapel exit.
- Turn-in point: Hold the line; open steering on exit.
- Apex: Inside kerb; flat by the last orange block.

### Turn 7
- Braking point: Brake after the 100m board; down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in as you slow; aim before first orange block.
- Apex: Inside kerb; full throttle by the last orange block.

### Turn 8
- Braking point: Brake just before the 100m board; down to 1st.
- Turn-in point: Early turn-in; hug the inside kerb.
- Apex: Tight left apex to open T9.

### Turn 9
- Braking point: Small lift to rotate.
- Turn-in point: Quick right turn; get back on power early.
- Apex: Use exit kerb to open T10.

### Turn 10
- Braking point: Flat; no braking.
- Turn-in point: Smooth right curve; avoid too much steering.
- Apex: Light inside kerb; keep within limits on exit.

### Turn 11
- Braking point: Lift progressively between the 100m and 50m boards.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for a late apex; keep car stable.
- Apex: Inside sausage kerbs; avoid over-using to prevent limits.

### Turn 12
- Braking point: Flat.
- Turn-in point: Smooth left; stay tight to open Village.
- Apex: Inside line; avoid running wide.

### Turn 13
- Braking point: Hard brake at the 100m board; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Start turning around the 50m board with trail brake.
- Apex: Tight apex; be slightly on throttle before apex.

### Turn 14
- Braking point: Brake at the green astroturf patch; down to 1st.
- Turn-in point: Turn in early; trail brake to rotate.
- Apex: Inside kerb; accelerate early for the straight.

### Turn 15
- Braking point: Flat.
- Turn-in point: Early turn-in; smooth steering.
- Apex: Cut the inside kerb and open the exit.

### Turn 16
- Braking point: Brake just after the 100m board (~75m); down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in tighter than expected with trail brake.
- Apex: Very late apex just after the 2nd orange block.

### Turn 17
- Braking point: Dab brake as the inside road ends; stay in 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in, let the car widen, then re-tighten.
- Apex: Third orange block on inside kerb; full throttle by the fourth.

### Turn 18
- Braking point: Flat.
- Turn-in point: Easy right curve; cut inside kerb.
- Apex: Inside kerb; run out to the left exit kerb.
