# Qatar - LMGT3 Track Guide

## Source Coverage
- Sources: https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/lusail-international-circuit-qatar-track-guide/, https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/the-2025-track-guide-list-for-le-mans-ultimate/
- Patch: Not stated in source (guide published 2025-07-11)
- Track layout: 16 turns

## Turn-by-Turn

### Turn 1
- Braking point: Brake around the 100m board; use the left entry kerb; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in as you trail brake from the left side.
- Apex: Inside kerb; unwind to the left exit curb near the gravel end.

### Turn 2
- Braking point: Brake half a car length after the right-side red/white curb; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in as you brake; mid-to-late apex.
- Apex: Inside curb; reach exit curb around its midpoint.

### Turn 3
- Braking point: Flat; no brake.
- Turn-in point: Smooth right bend; use inside kerb.
- Apex: Inside red/white curb; drift to left exit.

### Turn 4
- Braking point: Brake at the 3rd left-side marker; turn in immediately.
- Turn-in point: Trail brake into the right-hander.
- Apex: Close to orange inside surface without cutting.

### Turn 5
- Braking point: Lift or light brake only; use the gravel-end point as turn-in.
- Turn-in point: Turn in at the gravel-end reference on the left.
- Apex: Mid-to-late apex; use inside curb.

### Turn 6
- Braking point: Brake at/after the blue GoodYear board; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in as you trail brake; aim mid-apex.
- Apex: Inside kerb; unwind to the exit curb.

### Turn 7
- Braking point: Brake at the 2nd Bosch board; down to 2nd/3rd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for a late apex; be patient to rotate.
- Apex: Late apex; reach exit curb after it begins.

### Turn 8
- Braking point: Flat; stay on the left curb.
- Turn-in point: Hold the left-hand bend to open T9.
- Apex: Use the inside curb throughout.

### Turn 9
- Braking point: Lift or dab brake two car lengths before the left curb ends; down to 3rd/4th.
- Turn-in point: Short turn-in for the right-hander.
- Apex: Use inside curb; back to throttle as you leave it.

### Turn 10
- Braking point: Brake at the halfway point of the right-side access road; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in as the access road ends and blue paint starts.
- Apex: Inside curb at mid-point; power out within the white line.

### Turn 11
- Braking point: Flat; no braking.
- Turn-in point: Gentle left bend.
- Apex: Natural apex on inside curb; run to the end of the curb.

### Turn 12
- Braking point: Light brake after blue-to-orange paint change; 4th/5th.
- Turn-in point: Turn in at the color change; mid-apex.
- Apex: Inside curb; build throttle as you exit.

### Turn 13
- Braking point: Lift and possible dab; keep rolling speed.
- Turn-in point: Turn in at the gravel-end reference on the left.
- Apex: Short right apex; use inside curb.

### Turn 14
- Braking point: Lift and possibly light brake; patience for exit.
- Turn-in point: Turn in at the gravel-end point; slowest of the three rights.
- Apex: Inside curb; avoid running wide into gravel.

### Turn 15
- Braking point: Brake at or just after the Michelin board on the right; down one gear.
- Turn-in point: Turn in a car length after the brake reference; late apex.
- Apex: Inside curb; throttle just after apex.

### Turn 16
- Braking point: Brake just before the last Michelin board on the left; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in at the orange-to-maroon paint change; trail brake.
- Apex: Inside curb; power out to the long straight.
