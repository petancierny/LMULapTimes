# Circuit de la Sarthe - LMGT3 Track Guide

## Source Coverage
- Sources: https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/le-mans-ultimate-circuit-de-la-sarthe-le-mans-track-guide/, https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/the-2025-track-guide-list-for-le-mans-ultimate/
- Patch: Not stated in source (guide published 2024-11-08)
- Notes: Guide numbers turns through 33.
- Track layout: 33 turns

## Turn-by-Turn

### Turn 1
- Braking point: No brake for the fast right; set up on the left.
- Turn-in point: Turn in just before the pit exit white line ends.
- Apex: Inside kerb to straighten for T2.

### Turn 2
- Braking point: Hard brake as the road straightens; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in with the car straight; sacrifice entry speed.
- Apex: Cut the inside kerb and yellow sausage kerbs.

### Turn 3
- Braking point: Short brake or throttle burst to rotate.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for the right of the chicane.
- Apex: Inside kerb; manage traction on exit to the astroturf.

### Turn 4
- Braking point: Flat through the slight left; no braking.
- Turn-in point: Stay left to straighten T4.
- Apex: Short left bend; smooth steering.

### Turn 5
- Braking point: Flat; downhill right.
- Turn-in point: Hold a smooth right-hand line.
- Apex: Keep steering minimal to avoid scrub.

### Turn 6
- Braking point: Brake as the road straightens (white line reference); down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in early to the inside kerb.
- Apex: Hug the inside kerb without riding it.

### Turn 7
- Braking point: Carry from T6; no new heavy brake.
- Turn-in point: Aim to miss the last green bollard and cut the kerb.
- Apex: Inside blue/yellow kerb; straight-line the exit.

### Turn 8
- Braking point: Light brake at the outside kerb dip or before the grey projector box.
- Turn-in point: Commit to the right-hander with speed.
- Apex: Cut the inside kerb aggressively within limits.

### Turn 9
- Braking point: No brake; gentle right bend.
- Turn-in point: Shorten the bend by moving right then back left.
- Apex: Gentle right apex to straighten the Mulsanne exit.

### Turn 10
- Braking point: Brake hard just after the yellow marshal point; down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in to completely cut the inside.
- Apex: Inside kerb; open the entry for T11.

### Turn 11
- Braking point: Light brake; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Aim tight to the inside kerb.
- Apex: Just after the green bollard; balance throttle.

### Turn 12
- Braking point: Carry from T11; manage speed.
- Turn-in point: Let momentum take you wide then back in.
- Apex: Inside kerb; keep outside wheels off the kerb to avoid invalidation.

### Turn 13
- Braking point: Brake about two car lengths before the 100m board; down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for the left entry while trail braking.
- Apex: Cut the inside kerb close to the grass.

### Turn 14
- Braking point: Brake and downshift to 2nd for the right.
- Turn-in point: Rotate early for a straight exit.
- Apex: Apex just behind the green bollards.

### Turn 15
- Braking point: No extra heavy brake; carry momentum.
- Turn-in point: Hold the line with inside wheels on the kerb.
- Apex: Inside kerb; outside wheels on/over right kerb at exit.

### Turn 16
- Braking point: Brake just before blue/yellow kerb starts; start turning after 100m board.
- Turn-in point: Turn in to straighten braking for the hairpin.
- Apex: Right-hand bend apex; set up T17.

### Turn 17
- Braking point: Hard brake, down to 1st; trail brake into the hairpin.
- Turn-in point: Turn in aiming just after the last green bollard.
- Apex: Late apex; rotate to get early power for the straight.

### Turn 18
- Braking point: Flat; no braking.
- Turn-in point: Gentle right bend.
- Apex: Inside kerb to shorten the line.

### Turn 19
- Braking point: Flat; no braking.
- Turn-in point: Another right bend; keep steering minimal.
- Apex: Inside kerb; return left for Indianapolis.

### Turn 20
- Braking point: Brake while turning at the end of the green astroturf; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in with the car already facing the corner.
- Apex: Banked right; keep the car stable through the braking.

### Turn 21
- Braking point: Brake as you get parallel to the small right-side kerb.
- Turn-in point: Turn in about a car length after the short kerb begins.
- Apex: Mid inside kerb; avoid climbing too high.

### Turn 22
- Braking point: Brake before the GoodYear board; down to 1st.
- Turn-in point: Turn in just after the short left kerb starts.
- Apex: Kiss the inside kerb; avoid hopping over it.

### Turn 23
- Braking point: Light brake at the green rectangular patch; down to 4th.
- Turn-in point: Turn in as you cross the green block.
- Apex: Inside kerb start; keep the car tight.

### Turn 24
- Braking point: No heavy brake; flow to the left-hander.
- Turn-in point: Move right to open the left.
- Apex: Inside kerb just after the “31” board.

### Turn 25
- Braking point: Lift near the wall end; no heavy brake.
- Turn-in point: Turn in as the inside kerb starts.
- Apex: Inside kerb; back to throttle before the kerb ends.

### Turn 26
- Braking point: Light brake at the AVIS board; maintain momentum.
- Turn-in point: Turn in about a car length after the board.
- Apex: Inside kerb just after it begins.

### Turn 27
- Braking point: Lift before the recovery road; no hard brake.
- Turn-in point: Turn in as you lift; aim for the mid-corner sausage kerbs.
- Apex: Run inside wheels over the sausage kerbs; keep two wheels on exit kerb.

### Turn 28
- Braking point: Flat.
- Turn-in point: Short right; keep steering smooth.
- Apex: Inside kerb to shorten the line.

### Turn 29
- Braking point: Flat.
- Turn-in point: Left bend; keep inputs gentle.
- Apex: Inside kerb; set up Ford Chicanes.

### Turn 30
- Braking point: Brake ~2 car lengths before pit entry closes; down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for the left of the first chicane.
- Apex: Cut the inside kerb; avoid too much speed.

### Turn 31
- Braking point: Brake and downshift to 2nd before the right.
- Turn-in point: Turn in just after the inside board.
- Apex: Inside kerb; keep exit tight for the final chicane.

### Turn 32
- Braking point: Brake a car length before the outside kerb starts.
- Turn-in point: Trail brake into the left of the final chicane.
- Apex: Cut the inside yellow sausage kerb at mid-point.

### Turn 33
- Braking point: No extra heavy brake; focus on exit.
- Turn-in point: Aim for a slightly later apex in the final right.
- Apex: Use a touch of the raised sausage kerb; full throttle on exit.
