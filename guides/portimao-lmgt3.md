# Portimao - LMGT3 Track Guide

## Source Coverage
- Sources: https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/le-mans-ultimate-portimao-circuit-guide/, https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/the-2025-track-guide-list-for-le-mans-ultimate/
- Patch: Not stated in source (guide published 2024-11-08)
- Track layout: 15 turns

## Turn-by-Turn

### Turn 1
- Braking point: Brake just before the 100m board (near the solid white line); down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for a late apex, staying just behind the sausage curb.
- Apex: Late apex; avoid the sausage kerb; delay throttle until car is loaded.

### Turn 2
- Braking point: Flat; no braking.
- Turn-in point: Use mid track to open T3.
- Apex: Short right kink; no kerb strike.

### Turn 3
- Braking point: Brake at the midpoint between T2/T3 or near outer kerb; down to 1st.
- Turn-in point: Turn in to the tight right; keep the car stable.
- Apex: Late apex near the sausage curb without touching it.

### Turn 4
- Braking point: Flat uphill left; no braking.
- Turn-in point: Turn in at the solid white line across the track.
- Apex: Inside kerb; smooth steering over the crest.

### Turn 5
- Braking point: Brake at the 100m board on the right; down to 1st.
- Turn-in point: Trail brake into the downhill hairpin.
- Apex: Tight inside curb; straighten before throttle.

### Turn 6
- Braking point: Flat left kink; no braking.
- Turn-in point: Stay left for T7 entry.
- Apex: Short left kink; keep line tidy.

### Turn 7
- Braking point: Light brake just after the solid white line; keep gear.
- Turn-in point: Turn in and use inside curb; quick throttle to keep momentum.
- Apex: Inside red/white curb; set up for T8.

### Turn 8
- Braking point: Brake at the white line on the outside; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for a mid apex; smooth inputs.
- Apex: Mid apex; trail brake then feed throttle on exit.

### Turn 9
- Braking point: Flat; no braking.
- Turn-in point: Turn in before the right-side white line; smooth steering.
- Apex: Inside curb; prepare for T10/11.

### Turn 10
- Braking point: Brake at the marshal post on the left; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in as you brake up the hill.
- Apex: Right-hand apex near the last part of the double-right.

### Turn 11
- Braking point: Carry from T10; manage the blind crest.
- Turn-in point: Follow contour to the final right apex.
- Apex: Use red/white curb lightly; throttle around 50% then build.

### Turn 12
- Braking point: Flat left; no braking.
- Turn-in point: Mid-to-late apex; drift right for T13.
- Apex: Left apex; keep speed.

### Turn 13
- Braking point: Brake near the service road on the left; down two gears.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for a late apex; delay last downshift.
- Apex: Inside curb; avoid bumpy kerb; wide exit.

### Turn 14
- Braking point: Brake where the grass ends on the left; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Face the corner before braking; early apex.
- Apex: Early apex on inside curb; progressive throttle.

### Turn 15
- Braking point: Light brake or lift; fast right onto the straight.
- Turn-in point: Turn in about a car length before the inside kerb ends.
- Apex: Inside curb; slight lift, then full throttle to exit.
