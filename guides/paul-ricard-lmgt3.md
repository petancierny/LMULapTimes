# Paul Ricard - LMGT3 Track Guide

## Source Coverage
- Sources: https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/paul-ricard-track-guide-le-mans-ultimate/, https://news.racecontrol.gg/tracks/the-2025-track-guide-list-for-le-mans-ultimate/
- Patch: Not stated in source (guide published 2025-12-10)
- Notes: Guide text numbers turns through 17.
- Track layout: 17 turns

## Turn-by-Turn

### Turn 1
- Braking point: Brake just before the 100m board where the right kerb ends; down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in from the right with trail brake.
- Apex: Inside sausage kerb; aim to mount the inside curb.

### Turn 2
- Braking point: No heavy brake; smooth transition right.
- Turn-in point: Change direction smoothly to the right.
- Apex: Inside curb; avoid overusing to prevent bottoming out.

### Turn 3
- Braking point: Brake just after the 100m board; down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Use the 50m board as a turn-in reference.
- Apex: Cut the inside curb; keep exit tidy for T4.

### Turn 4
- Braking point: Add brake while downshifting to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in for the left; commit or avoid the kerb.
- Apex: Inside kerb; avoid half-committed kerb usage.

### Turn 5
- Braking point: Brake just after the left curb ends; down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Late-apex mindset.
- Apex: Inside painted curb; avoid climbing it.

### Turn 6
- Braking point: Brake just after the 100m board; down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Trail brake into the first right of Ste Baume.
- Apex: Subtle apex; rotate across the inside edge.

### Turn 7
- Braking point: Carry from T6; no heavy braking.
- Turn-in point: Re-rotate for the second right.
- Apex: Second apex with a touch more curb; build throttle to exit.

### Turn 8
- Braking point: Brake hard at the 100m board; down to 2nd/3rd.
- Turn-in point: Late turn-in for the left of the chicane.
- Apex: Inside curb; avoid launching over it.

### Turn 9
- Braking point: Transition right with the car settled.
- Turn-in point: Quick direction change; keep stability.
- Apex: Small amount of inside curb; focus on exit.

### Turn 10
- Braking point: Usually flat or a small lift depending on conditions.
- Turn-in point: Use left curb or marshal light as reference.
- Apex: Skim the inside curb; let the car run wide on exit.

### Turn 11
- Braking point: Brake just after the road dip; down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Trail brake into the first Beausset apex.
- Apex: Light clip of the first apex; focus on rotation.

### Turn 12
- Braking point: No extra heavy brake; coast to re-rotate.
- Turn-in point: Aim for the tighter second apex.
- Apex: Second apex on painted curb; smooth throttle out.

### Turn 13
- Braking point: Brake at the start of the right-side curb; down to 3rd.
- Turn-in point: Attack the first right of Bendor.
- Apex: Inside curb; quick change to the left.

### Turn 14
- Braking point: Carry speed; avoid overusing the left curb.
- Turn-in point: Flick left; stay smooth.
- Apex: Left apex with minimal curb to avoid pushing wide.

### Turn 15
- Braking point: Brake just after the 50m board; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Turn in later than expected.
- Apex: Tight inside curb; avoid running out wide.

### Turn 16
- Braking point: Light brake; shallow apex for positioning.
- Turn-in point: Turn in from the left; keep wheel straight for early throttle.
- Apex: Shallow apex; focus on line to Pont.

### Turn 17
- Braking point: Brake just before the end of the left curb; down to 2nd.
- Turn-in point: Assertive turn-in; ride the inside curb.
- Apex: Inside curb; open hands early for the straight.
